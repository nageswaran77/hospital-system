About MediCare

Dedicated to Excellence in Healthcare

Our History

Founded in 1998, MediCare started as a small clinic with a big vision: to provide accessible, high-quality healthcare to the community. Over the past 25+ years, we have grown into a multi-specialty hospital equipped with the latest technology and a team of world-renowned specialists.

We have consistently been recognized for our excellence in patient care, innovative medical treatments, and community outreach programs.

Our Mission

To improve the health and well-being of the communities we serve by providing compassionate, high-quality, and accessible healthcare services.

Our Vision

To be the region's premier healthcare provider, recognized for our excellence in medical care, innovation, and patient satisfaction.
